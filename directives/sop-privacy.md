# SOP: Privacy and Data Handling

## Data Minimization
- Store only lead data needed for qualification and handoff.
- Avoid storing unnecessary personal details.

## Masking
- Mask phone and email in logs.
- Full contact data may exist in CRM payloads only.

## Retention
- Keep chat transcripts for operational analytics only.
- Allow per-client retention windows in configuration.

## Compliance Guardrails
- Do not fabricate legal or pricing guarantees.
- Escalate to human when user requests binding commitments.
